Data visualisation

The data visualisation tools present facts and figures about a range of occupational safety and health (OSH) issues across Europe, including how risks are managed, working conditions, demographics, health impact, policies and strategies.

The user-friendly tools display key findings and data that can be filtered by country, sector and other variables.
